Rose

Benefits: 

                1.Garden Decor: Widely used in gardens for their beauty.
                2.Floral Arrangements: Common in bouquets and centerpieces.
                3.Perfume Industry: Essential oils used in fragrances.
                4.Skincare Products:Ingredients in lotions and creams for their soothing properties.
                5.Culinary Uses: Rose water and petals in desserts and beverages.
                6.Medicinal Uses: Rose hips for vitamin C and teas.
                7.Symbolism: Represent love, beauty, and passion.
                8.Crafts: Used in potpourri and handmade soaps.
                9.Religious Ceremonies: Incorporated in rituals and offerings.
                10.Literature and Art: Symbols in poems, stories, and visual arts

Additional Key Points About Roses:

                    1.Pest Control: Natural deterrent for nematodes and other garden pests.
                    2.Companion Planting: Planted alongside vegetables to repel harmful insects.
                    3.Medicinal Uses: Used in traditional remedies for skin conditions and wounds.
                    4.Culinary Uses: Edible petals used in salads and as food coloring.
                    5.Cosmetics: Extracts used in skincare products for their anti-inflammatory properties.
                    6.Dye Production: Flowers used to produce natural dyes for fabrics.
                    7.Aromatherapy: Essential oils used for their soothing and healing properties.
                    8.Pollinator Attraction: Attracts bees and other pollinators to gardens.
